Robert Lennox
PLS (Oregon LS 2886, Washington LS 38030)

Robert has twenty years of surveying experience since receiving his certificate in Civil and Surveying Technologies from Albuquerque Technical and Vocational Institute in 1988. He was licensed in Oregon in 1998 and in Washington in 2001. Since forming Bluedot Group in 2002 Robert has specialized in managing public works surveying projects. His consulting talents, combined with proficient use of survey equipment, data processing software, and AutoCAD customization skills, have enabled him to provide high quality work in a responsive manner for his clients.
